Sensing the dark intention in this creature of light you turn and flee, back towards the tunnel, which appears as a dark hole, a hopeful spot in a rainbow of terror.

As you feel the creature growing behind you, flooding the cave with brightness, you bound forward on all fours, your paws beating the ground.

Before you reach the hole you can't help but look at what is pursuing you. You catch only a glimpse; a mouth, giant and luminous and then it closes around you leaving only...
