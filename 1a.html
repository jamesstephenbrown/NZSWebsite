And that's when it happened, she heard the familiar tinkle of Hedges, her neighbour's cat. She unconsciously dropped her bike and wandered, as if floating, through the gate towards the sound. Hedges was always so friendly, and as Mina knelt down Hedges darted towards her.

Hedges rolled around on the berm playing with Mina, rubbing up to her, circling her legs, galloping along the path, climbing up a tree, Mina following like a loyal companion, almost completely forgetting about the very ordinary day that had been in store for her. When she heard the faint call of her father as Hedges turned down a neighbour's long driveway, Mina decided to...

  • ... follow Hedges
  • ... to go back into the garden and wait for Dad
