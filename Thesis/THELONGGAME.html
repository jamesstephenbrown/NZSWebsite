THE LONG GAME

Long term, paradigm shifts such as the spherical earth model would be necessary for explorers and eventually the space program. Our current understanding of Quantum mechanics is vital for, GPS, MRI machines, lasers, telecommunications, atomic clocks and the modern semi-conductors in computers and smart phones. It could one day revolutionise computing with the much promised Quantum Computer! Long term, these technologies will help us to gain a greater understanding of consciousness which might help us produce more ethical societies.
