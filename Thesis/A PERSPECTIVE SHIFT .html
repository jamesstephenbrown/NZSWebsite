A PERSPECTIVE SHIFT

Conceding that Free Will is an illusion and yet living as if we and others have free will may be a useful heuristic - given that profound truths make little difference. However, acknowledging that free will is a fiction allows us to look at particular cases anew. There may be particular cases where an emphasis on data about outcomes rather than traditional ideas about individual responsibility might turn out to not only be more accurate but also be more pragmatic.
