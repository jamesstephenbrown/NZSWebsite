AND WHAT IF WE DON'T CHANGE?

If we fail to look at the world this way, we will create the negative sum fate we fear. There is a part of our zero-sum thinking that expects systems to self-balance, to navigate about zero, tending toward equity. We are, after all, surrounded by a world full of self-balancing systems. But not all systems tend towards balance, in fact, only old systems do this - and have hence survived. New systems, like capitalism, fossil fuels, or technology in general, could easily move in the opposite direction. Compound interest, leverage and political influence lead capital to concentrate rather than disperse, increases in carbon dioxide create higher temperatures, leading to higher levels of carbon dioxide, fueling runaway climate change, even our technology might get away on us.


SO, WE JUST NEED TO MAINTAIN BALANCE?
