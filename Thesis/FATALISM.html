FATALISM

Conceding that Free Will in some respects is illusory does not mean to say that reality is a farce and you can throw up our hands and sit on the couch. First of all fatalism is undermined by randomness, in that randomness makes the future unpredictable, so your fate is not written. Secondly, as I understand it, while much of what we perceive as our unified self is illusory, our conscious decision-making process is not irrelevant.
