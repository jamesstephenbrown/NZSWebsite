WHAT IS A PERPETUAL MOTION MACHINE?

A perpetual motion machine is an entity that moves continuously, potentially forever, with the capacity to output energy without having to take in an equal or greater amount of energy in order to maintain motion.

A perpetual motion machine is the holy grail of physics, in that it is both a cure all for the worldâ€™s energy needs and also a complete fantasy.

It has long been known that perpetual motion machines cannot exist, because they contradict one or both of the first 2 laws of Thermodynamics.
