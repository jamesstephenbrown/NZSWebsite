LIBET’S REAR VISION MIRROR

Recent research by Benjamin Libet has demonstrated that decisions precede conscious awareness with one caveat. The concept of decisions preceding conscious awareness was not new, and had been toyed with by western behaviourists in the mid 20th century and by various eastern religious traditions. It is illustrated nicely by the theologian and “Guru” Alan Watts when he described consciousness as “driving the car with eyes glued to the rear vision mirror”. Are we really driving at all?
