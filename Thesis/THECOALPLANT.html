A STORY ABOUT A COAL PLANT

If a coal powerstation can see that their financial advantage is being paid for by the whole of humanity via environmental destruction, they can trust that their benefits are real. They can see clearly the cost verses benefit equation. They can see that the sum total advantage is zero or less than zero, and they can also see that they are not having to pay their fair share of the cost, meaning their total advantage is positive or otherwise known as “fiscally responsible”.
