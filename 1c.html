Mina was interrupted by a low rumbling, and as she stepped turned to investigate, she let her bike fall carelessly to the ground, as she clinked the gate open she was faced with the source of the rumbling, a rumbling that had become a growl.

In the middle of the road was a beastly-looking dog, slathering over a piece of broken wood and shaking it from side to side, the loose skin on its face contorting with the motion. Then as if she came into view the dog locked eyes with her. The dog dropped the wood and began to bound towards her.

Mina fumbled with the latch, but as dog leapt, she had no choice but to go...
