Mina was interrupted by a low rumbling, and as she stepped turned to investigate, she let her bike fall carelessly to the ground, as she clinked the gate open she was faced with the source of the rumbling, a rumbling that had become a growl.

In the middle of the road was a beastly-looking dog, slathering over a piece of broken wood and shaking it from side to side, the loose skin on its face contorting with the motion. She knew this, his name had been bellowed at the top of some large man's voice for all the street to hear many a time.
"Precious, PRECIOUS, blym'n 'ek Precious, get inside!"
But there was no voice today to call the dog away today, as the dog locked eyes with Mina. The dog dropped the wood and began to bound towards her. Mina fumbled with the latch, but as dog leapt, she had no choice but to go...
