You bound after the colourful creature, trailing a rainbow in its wake. As it darts this way and that, you notice the cave has a life to it, the plants sway and respond as if an audience to a show.

Even the dark ground beneath your feet feels soft and a pulse of light enters the surface as you gallop. And then, as if by instinct, you pounce, and clasp the butterfly between your front paws.

Then everything changes, the room glows with an angry intensity, you drop the butterfly and see it begin to grow, you turn and...

  • ... run!
