She turned in an instant and dashed as fast as she could, down the street, looking behind her as the dog clumsily reoriented itself and bounded after her. She looked ahead as the barking got louder behind her.

Until there was an almighty yelp. Mina skidded to a halt and turned around to see nothing, no dog, only a distant echoey howl. She walked back towards sound and in front of her was revealed a perfectly circular hole, lined in gold, with the faint impression of a ladder inside, descending into the darkness.
