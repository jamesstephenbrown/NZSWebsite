non-ze·ro-sum (non-zîr′ō-sŭm′, non-zē′rō-)

adj. Of or relating to a situation in which a gain is not offset by an equal loss



Non-zero-sum games are a central element of Game Theory, and while this site does not represent a primer on Game Theory, I hope to take game theoretical principles and explore real world dynamics through the use of games and simulations.

Many of my experiments are hosted at the fabulous indie game site itch.io and if you would like to explore them all without all the writing visit...

Something I've been experimenting with is an interactive essay format, which generates a self-organising node map which depicts the relationships between ideas as you explore them.
