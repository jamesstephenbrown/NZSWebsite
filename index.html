non-ze·ro-sum (non-zîr′ō-sŭm′, non-zē′rō-)

adj. Of or relating to a situation in which a gain is not offset by an equal loss


Non-zero-sum games are a central element of Game Theory, and has been a fascination of mine for a couple of decades. While this site does not represent a primer on Game Theory, I hope to take game theoretical principles and explore real world dynamics through the use of games and simulations.
