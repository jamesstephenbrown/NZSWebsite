When Mina got back the gate her dog Darwin was standing to attention at the top of the porch steps. As the gate latch clinked upon Mina's entry, Darwin let out an almighty howl and greyhounded toward her, barking. Sensing what might be attracting such attention she turned to see the cat heckles up, hissing in response, and before she had a chance Darwin was off after the poor cat.

Mina now raced after the dog, racing after the cat. As she rounded the bend she saw an ominous sight the dog peering over what looked like a man-hole, but with no lid, perfectly circular, with a golden rim. Sitting next to the dog she could see faint ladder rungs fading into the darkness of the well, and could hear the echo of an unmistakable...

"MEEOW"... and then silence.

It couldn't possibly be very far down but she could hear her dad calling out, Darwin scampered off back toward the distant voice, but Mina

  • ... reached her foot in, and began to carefully descend the ladder
